
Privacy Policy

Last updated: 8th February 2025

Poke ShowStats respects the privacy of its users. This application does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to any external server.

Data Collection and Usage
All data generated by the application is stored locally on the user's device. No information is sent to a remote server, shared with third parties, or used for analytics or advertising purposes.

Permissions
The application requires the INTERNET permission, but it is not used to transmit any user data. It is solely needed for fetching Pokemon Showdown replays.

Security
Since all data remains stored on the device, the user is responsible for its security and backup.

Changes
This privacy policy may be updated in the future. Any changes will be reflected in this section.
